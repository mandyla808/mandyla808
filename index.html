About Me

Life of the Mind, Life of the Grind: the UChicago motto that I find myself unironically living by. I entered UChicago as a Math major, passionate about wrapping my head around abstract theories and developing elegant proofs. I then discovered the magic of coding in my first CS course. I really enjoy using the problem solving skills I've developed in math classes to build interesting and useful programs! Now I am pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics from UChicago. I am going into my 4th year this fall, and expect to graduate in June 2021.
